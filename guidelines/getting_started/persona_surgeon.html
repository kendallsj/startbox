Surgeon Persona

Samantha Female, Age 43

"I don’t have much time to learn new technology – I need something simple that makes my job easier, not more difficult.”

Profile:

  • MD orthopedic surgeon for 18 years
  • Works in different hospitals or other facilities throughout the week

Goals:

  • Help educate patients so they can understand their injuries and what is causing them pain
  • Recommend a course of action, whether it be surgery, exercise, or otherwise to help fix the problem
  • Make sure the patients are getting the proper care leading up to, during, and after their procedure(s)

Typical Day:

She often works a 10-12 hour day. Some days she is at the clinical office diagnosing and educating patients. Shortly after, she will dictate the patient demographics and procedure, handing it off to a nurse or scribe to enter it into the EHR. Other days she will go to a hospital or surgery center to make final verifications with patients in pre-op before performing surgery on them.

Pain Points:

  • Finds it difficult to dictate notes after confirming the procedure with the patient
  • Using multiple applications to enter and dictate and notes
  • Sometimes learns the procedure/laterality is incorrect in pre-op, right before surgery
